LAB51 / R&D



A speculative design laboratory operating at the edge of design, computation, and urban futures.


LAB51 explores architecture as a system, rather than an object. We prototype spatial intelligence through AI, simulation, collaboration and research-driven design.



ETHOS

Architecture is no longer static. It is adaptive, data-driven, and continuously evolving.



MISSION

To construct new design models for a rapidly transforming world.


METHODOLOGY

A. Observation & Research

Understanding context, environment, and social needs to inform meaningful experiments.

B. Ideation & Experimentation

Testing unconventional approaches using AI, parametric design, and material prototypes.

C. Iteration & Testing

Refining ideas through simulation, feedback loops, and performance evaluation.

D. Application & Knowledge sharing

Documenting findings to inspire real-world design, collaborators, and future research.




RESEARCH FOCUS

Our research explores experimental approaches that could transform the residential industry. Our current focus areas include:

R.01_ Circular Materials & Adaptive Reuse

Investigating low-carbon materials, modular construction, and reconfigurable apartment units for sustainable, long-life buildings.

R.02_ AI-Driven Human-Centric Design

Using generative design and predictive analytics to optimise apartment layouts, social interaction, and occupant wellness in high-density environments.

R.03_ Residual space

Identify, map, and explore activation of residual space in Urban setting.
